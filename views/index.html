About Me

Hi, I'm Spencer Nakamura, a passionate photographer based in Vancouver. Photography is more than just a hobby for me—it's a way to tell stories through the lens.

My journey into photography began at community events, where I volunteered to capture the energy and moments shared by brilliant people. Over the years, I have honed my skills in event photography, headshots, and street photography.

I love capturing genuine emotions, candid moments, and the beauty of everyday life. Whether I’m covering a large event or shooting a quiet portrait session, I always strive to create images that feel authentic and memorable.

If you're looking for a photographer who’s passionate about storytelling through images, feel free to browse my portfolio and reach out for bookings.
